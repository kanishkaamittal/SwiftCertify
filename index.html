Our Services

Rewarding recipients with a digital verifiable Certificate made simple in 5 steps:

At Give My Certificate, our mission is to ensure the authenticity of hard-earned documents, eliminating the possibility of forgery. We've streamlined the entire process to make it remarkably seamless. Here's how it works:

Step 1: Provide Information - The document issuing party submits an excel sheet containing recipients' relevant information along with the desired certificate design.

Step 2: Document Generation - Our platform leverages the provided data and certificate design to generate digital certificates, ensuring accuracy and consistency.

Step 3: Verification Integration - Each certificate is embedded with verification features, making it easy to verify authenticity on-demand.

Step 4: Digital Convenience - Digital certificates offer inherent benefits such as easy storage, accessibility, and the ability to quickly share them with others.

Step 5: Consolidated Dashboard - All generated certificates are consolidated in a user-friendly dashboard, providing a comprehensive overview of the document issuance process.
